Explore Tucson's Best Family-Friendly Activities

Tucson is brimming with activities perfect for families. From interactive museums to wildlife experiences, there’s something for every age group to enjoy!

  • Biosphere 2: A one-of-a-kind research facility showcasing ecosystems like rainforests, deserts, and oceans under a massive dome. Perfect for families to explore science and sustainability through guided tours and interactive exhibits.
  • Pima Air and Space Museum: One of the largest aerospace museums in the world, featuring over 400 aircraft and interactive exhibits.
  • Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum: A mix of zoo, botanical garden, and museum, showcasing live desert animals and scenic trails.
  • Tucson Botanical Gardens: A serene oasis offering butterfly exhibits, lush gardens, and educational art installations.
  • Rooster Cogburn Ostrich Ranch: A hands-on animal experience where you can feed ostriches, goats, and lorikeets.
  • USAF Airplane Boneyard: The world’s largest aircraft storage facility with guided tours and historical insights.
  • Reid Park Zoo: Features exotic animals, interactive exhibits, and a kid-friendly play area for young adventurers.
