Explore Tucson's Best Family-Friendly Activities

Tucson is brimming with activities perfect for families. From interactive museums to wildlife experiences, there’s something for every age group to enjoy!

Biosphere 2

https://biosphere2.org/

A one-of-a-kind research facility showcasing ecosystems like rainforests, deserts, and oceans under a massive dome. Perfect for families to explore science and sustainability through guided tours and interactive exhibits.

Pima Air and Space Museum

https://pimaair.org/

One of the largest aerospace museums in the world, featuring over 400 aircraft and interactive exhibits.

Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

https://www.desertmuseum.org/

A mix of zoo, botanical garden, and museum, showcasing live desert animals and scenic trails.

Tucson Botanical Gardens

https://tucsonbotanical.org/

A serene oasis offering butterfly exhibits, lush gardens, and educational art installations.

Rooster Cogburn Ostrich Ranch

https://www.roostercogburn.com/

A hands-on animal experience where you can feed ostriches, goats, and lorikeets.

USAF Airplane Boneyard

https://www.dm.af.mil/Visit/Boneyard/

The world’s largest aircraft storage facility with guided tours and historical insights.

Reid Park Zoo

https://reidparkzoo.org/

Features exotic animals, interactive exhibits, and a kid-friendly play area for young adventurers.
